Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is included in the custom training programs?

Custom training programs are tailored specifically to your fitness goals, experience level, and available equipment. They include detailed workout plans, progress tracking, and video demonstrations if necessary.

2. How do I get started with online coaching?

You can get started by filling out the contact form or reaching out via email. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your goals and determine the best coaching package for you.

3. Do you offer nutrition plans?

While I focus on training programs and coaching, I can provide general advice and recommendations. However, I do not create detailed nutrition plans as part of my services.

4. What equipment do I need for the programs?

Most programs are designed to be completed with basic gym equipment, such as a barbell, dumbbells, and resistance bands. If you have specific limitations, I can adjust the program to suit your setup.

5. Can beginners join your programs?

Absolutely! My programs cater to all levels, from beginners to advanced athletes. Each program is tailored to your experience and fitness level.
